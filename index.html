About Me

A fervent tinkerer, I am interested in medical devices, robotics, and prosthetics. I completed my B.S. in Computer Science at Tufts University in Medford, Massachusetts. I then completed the Master's in Research (MRes) in Medical Robotics in The Hamlyn Centre at Imperial College London. Between September 2022 and November 2023, I was a PhD student in Mechanical Engineering at Boston University.
Since November 2023, I am a PhD student in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Leeds.
